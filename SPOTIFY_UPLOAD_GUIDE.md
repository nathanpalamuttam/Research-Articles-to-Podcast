# How to Listen to Your Podcasts on Spotify

## The Problem
Spotify doesn't allow direct uploads of custom audio files. You need a podcast hosting service.

## Solutions (Ranked by Ease)

### Option 1: Use Anchor (Free & Easiest) âœ… RECOMMENDED
Anchor is owned by Spotify and automatically distributes to Spotify.

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://anchor.fm
2. Sign up for free account
3. Click "New Episode"
4. Upload your MP3 files from `outputs/audio/`
5. Publish episodes
6. Anchor automatically submits to Spotify (takes 24-48 hours)
7. Search for your podcast on Spotify mobile app

**Pros:**
- Free
- Automatic Spotify distribution
- No technical setup
- Mobile app for easy uploads

**Cons:**
- Manual upload for each episode
- 24-48 hour delay

---

### Option 2: Use Google Podcasts / Apple Podcasts (Free)
Upload to Google Drive or Dropbox, create RSS feed, submit to podcast directories.

**Steps:**
1. Upload MP3s to Google Drive/Dropbox (public)
2. Use a service like Podbean or Buzzsprout (free tier)
3. Submit RSS feed to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts

---

### Option 3: Self-Host with RSS Feed (Technical)
Create your own RSS feed and submit to Spotify.

**Requirements:**
- Web hosting with public access
- RSS feed generator
- Submit feed to Spotify for Podcasters

**I can help you build this if you want!**

---

### Option 4: Local Playback (Immediate, but Phone Only)
For immediate listening without uploading:

**On iPhone:**
1. Use AirDrop to send MP3 to your phone
2. Save to Files app
3. Play in Apple Podcasts or VLC

**On Android:**
1. Use Google Drive or Dropbox
2. Download MP3
3. Play in any podcast app (Pocket Casts, etc.)
